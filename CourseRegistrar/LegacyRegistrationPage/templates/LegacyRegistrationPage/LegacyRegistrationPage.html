Students that select hybrid or in-person courses agree to follow the University policy for face coverings and other physical mitigation measures that reduce the transmission of COVID-19 while present in the classroom and other University facilities. More information can be found here: doyourpart.ucmerced.edu

To add a class, enter the Course Reference Number (CRN) in the "Add Classes Worksheet" section. If you have already registered for the semester, those classes will appear in the "Current Schedule" section. Additional classes may be added in the "Add Classes Worksheet".

To drop a class, use the options available in the "Action" field drop down menu. If no options are listed in the "Action" field then the class may not be dropped.

When add/drops are complete click Submit Changes. If you are unsure of which classes to add, go to registrar.ucmerced.edu/go/schedule.

NOTE: DO NOT USE THE CLASS SEARCH BUTTON BELOW TO SEARCH FOR CLASS SECTIONS! It does not clearly show linked discussion or lab sections that go with lecture sections. Instead use registrar.ucmerced.edu/go/schedule.

Be aware of all add and drop deadlines each semester. For update information see the Office of the Registrar web site.

Need extra help registering for classes? Go to the registration help web site.

Error messages could include time conflicts, prerequisites, linking, instructor permission, etc. More information about error messages and how to correct them can be found here.

We recommend you attempt to complete registration within two hours of your registration start time to reduce the amount of registration activity occurring throughout the day.

***IMPORTANT REGISTRATION UPDATE - ELECTRONIC FORM SUBMISSION DURING COVID-19***
Given the move to remote instruction, you can now download forms you may need to finalize your registration, complete the form and email out for approval via your UC Merced email. More information can be found on the Registrar Website see at registrar.ucmerced.edu/forms.
